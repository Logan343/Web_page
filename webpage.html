ABOUT THE EVENT

It aims to incise the sharp minds of college students to explore the world of ENVIRONMENTAL ENTREPRENEURSHIP by deducing a proper technical solution to an issue native to their institute's city and creating their own business model out of it.

Ecopreneur is chance to represent your college, it is an opportunity to solve a real world problem, a platform to represent a vision in front of notable people, a medium to win unimaginable prizes and recognition and a direction to maybe even get a fixed career path to follow out of the plan you create!

EVENT STRUCTURE

PHASE 1: Ecowiz


A quiz centered around the topic of ecological sustainability will be conducted in all the participating colleges. Top 5 candidates from each college will be chosen to form a team and represent their respective college.

PHASE 2: Drafting of the Solution and Business Model


In this intermediate phase, two problem statements will be sent to each team which relate to the local environmental problems in the region where their college is situated. Out of the two, teams will be required to select any one problem statement and build a business model around it. The time duration for this phase will be one month. The teams will have to send us their business models within the stipulated time limit.

PHASE 3: Presenting and Pitching


The 3rd phase will take place in Consortium '22. In this round, the best performing teams, shortlisted on the basis of their business models, will be invited to the VNIT campus. These teams will have to pitch in front of a panel of judges convincing how their business model would be an efficient step to bring about a positive change to solve the environmental problems faced in that region. Based on the worthiness and impact of the idea, the judges will then choose a winner.

ASSOCIATION

Ecopreneur is proudly conducted in association with the PAN NIT ALUMNI FORUM(PNAF), an umbrella organization, covering alumni associations and thereby alumni of all NITs, to evolve a brand "NIT" that would provide strong fraternity.

PNAF and E-CELL VNIT share common interests like innovation and entrepreneurship along with holistic development for responsible citizens.
